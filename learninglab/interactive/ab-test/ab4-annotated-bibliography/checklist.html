Annotated bibliography checklist

Use the checklist to identify key parts of the example annotated bibliography.

Example

Johnson, L. M., & Carver, N. (2021). Virtual collaboration: Exploring effectiveness and productivity. Journal of Digital Work Studies, 15(3), 45-60. https://doi.org/10.1080/jdws.2021.5678

Summary

The article investigates how virtual collaboration tools impact team productivity and effectiveness. The main aim is to understand the role of digital platforms in enhancing remote team communication and efficiency. Supporting arguments include technology proficiency and team dynamics as key factors for effective collaboration. Methods include surveys and interviews with remote workers across various industries, revealing that well-integrated digital tools significantly enhance team performance.

Critical Analysis

The article provides a thorough analysis of strengths explained using robust data collection methods and comprehensive statistical interpretations. The inclusion of a large, diverse participant pool strengthens the findings' generalisability. However, the study's limitation lies in its focus on technology-intensive industries, which may skew results. Additionally, potential bias is introduced through self-reported data weaknesses.

Reflection

Johnson and Carver's work is crucial for understanding the nuances of virtual collaboration, offering insights applicable to my research on remote team management. However, the industry-specific focus requires consideration of its applicability to other fields, which might limit its usefulness.
