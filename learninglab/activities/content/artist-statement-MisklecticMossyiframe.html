Misklectic and Mossy 333
I’m Here: Portrait of Mossy Jade Johnson, 2017
Single channel HD video, Duration: 3:56 minutes
RMIT University Gallery

Our friendship started in art school, our early experimentation was Mossy's first exploration in performative movement. Mossy states this was an important moment in her coming into womanhood and comfort in her body.
In this work, I have translated Mossy's spoken word poem into a digital portrait. It has been an opportunity to demystify and humanise the concept of a trans-woman and to show the complexity of her identity, be it objectified, demonised, hyper-sexualised in her vulnerability, beauty, and strength. Through our intimate exchange via the lens, this work contains visual symbols that reference our historical collaborations and celebrates our friendship.
